About Me

Hey there! I’m Keely — a digital marketer by day and full-stack-developer-in-training by night… and day. I am currently a student in the UT Houston Full-Stack Developer Program and hope to grow my career through applying a strategic marketing lens with my technical skills in an innovative startup environment.

Upon graduating with a double major in Marketing and Management Entrepreneurship from the University of Wisconsin-Madison, I began my career in consumer insights. Since, I’ve evolved my career to specialize in the direction of the industry — online. From drafting, deploying and digesting the data, I manage a range of online activations including digital advertising, email marketing, and social media for a growing national hospitality company.

When I’m not glued to my screen, I'm guilty of finding solace in a variety of “basic” cliche activities — yoga, running, traveling, and pretending like I can cook gourmet vegetarian meals by following the instructions provided with my recent Blue Apron delivery.
Cheers!
